🌈 What Keeps Me Going

Here are the things that inspire me every day to keep moving forward.

🎯 Personal Goals

I'm driven by a desire to improve myself and become better at what I love doing — especially fixing and understanding technology. I want to one day build something entirely my own, whether it's a small business, a tool, or a platform that helps others.

🧠 Lifelong Learning

Learning doesn’t stop in school. I always try to learn new skills — especially when it comes to tech, creativity, and things I can use with my hands. It’s the process of figuring things out that excites me the most.

💚 People I Care About

One big reason I keep going is the people close to me — family, friends, and those who support me in quiet ways. I want to make them proud and be someone they can count on.

🌱 Growth Mindset

Even if it’s slow, I believe in progress. Small steps matter. I know that as long as I keep showing up and trying, I’ll get better — and that’s enough to push me forward.
